About Us

ORC Engineering is an integrated engineering services and manufacturing company operating in India. We are in the business of delivering client-specific solutions for projects related to Networking, Infrastructure, Solar Energy, Electrical & Electronics industry. The integrated package of services encompasses engineering, procurement, manufacturing, construction, commissioning and maintenance ORC Engineering is a multinational company renowned globally for its quality, safety and on-time delivery of projects and products. ORC Engineering specializes in design consultancy, civil design, electrical design, mechanical design and Engineering management. We are experts in EPC, Construction, Maintenance/Industrial Services, Infrastructure and Manufacturing. All the faculties required to deliver turnkey projects, reside within the company. This unique strength enables us to provide solutions as EPCC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction and Commissioning) services provider to a host of international clients

Our Journey



The journey began, in a six hundred square feet office space at Kolkata We had a vision to embark on a journey to create India’s leading engineering enterprise. Over the years, the spirit of our founders inspired our people to not just achieve the ordinary but the extraordinary.

This belief became the India’s leading multinational company – ORC. As part of our Corporate Social Responsibility, ORC takes immense pride in educating underprivileged youth, keen to acquire technical skills and improving their livelihood

We also provides vocational trainings to young aspiring students. Driven by a set vision and commitment, ORC strives to add value to our clients’ businesses by providing world-class solutions at cost-effective levels.
